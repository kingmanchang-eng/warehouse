Built for Heavy-Duty Warehouse Operations

The RBL-FK series is engineered to replace manual forklift operations in high-throughput distribution centers. With intelligent path planning and robust obstacle avoidance, it integrates seamlessly into existing workflows without requiring infrastructure modifications.

Optimized for High-Density Storage

The RBL-ST500 is engineered specifically for automated storage and retrieval systems (ASRS). Its ultra-compact chassis and specialized lifting mast allow it to operate in aisles as narrow as 1.2 meters, maximizing warehouse cube utilization. Hybrid navigation ensures flawless operation between dense racking structures where laser lines-of-sight are frequently obstructed.

Client Background

The client is a leading regional FMCG distributor operating three major temperature-controlled facilities across Southeast Asia, managing a total floor space of 120,000 m².

Prior to automation, the facility relied on 180 manual operators working across a 3-shift, 24/7 schedule. The extreme cold environment (-25°C) led to high worker fatigue, frequent picking errors, and significant challenges in labor retention, threatening their ability to meet growing regional demand.

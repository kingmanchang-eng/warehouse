Built for Versatile Material Handling

The RBL-LF1000 represents the pinnacle of mid-tier autonomous material transport. Designed to bridge the gap between heavy payload movement and high-speed sortation, this unit integrates seamlessly with existing warehouse infrastructure.

Featuring a zero-degree turning radius and robust 48V power architecture, it excels in narrow aisles and dense storage environments where traditional forklifts struggle. The advanced Laser SLAM navigation ensures centimeter-level accuracy even in dynamic, constantly shifting warehouse layouts.

15,000-Pallet ASRS in a -30°C Cold Chain Facility

How RoboLyne delivered a fully automated storage & retrieval system for temperature-sensitive goods in 10 weeks.

15,000 Pallet Positions
3x Storage Density
10-Week Go-Live
99.6% Order Accuracy

Client Background & Context

Operating at the critical intersection of food safety and rapid logistics, this leading Southeast Asian cold chain distributor faced a defining inflection point. With consumer demand for frozen FMCG products surging across the region, their existing infrastructure—reliant on manual forklift operations within harsh, -30°C environments—was struggling to maintain the necessary throughput and accuracy.

The scale of their operation required a fundamental shift in storage philosophy. They needed to maximize vertical space utilization while removing human operators from extreme temperature zones, all without disrupting ongoing, round-the-clock distribution activities.
